Champorado: A Filipino Chocolate Rice Porridge

Craving a comforting and delicious snack? Look no further than Champorado! This Filipino classic is a warm and creamy porridge made with glutinous rice and rich cocoa, perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth. It's a delightful way to start your day or indulge in a cozy afternoon treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous sweet rice (also known as sticky rice)
  • 2 cups light coconut milk
  • Â½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup white sugar (adjust to your sweetness preference)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Combine the glutinous rice and light coconut milk in a pot. Bring to a boil for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  2. Reduce heat to low, then stir in the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt. Cover the pot and continue cooking for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice becomes tender. Add the thick coconut milk and stir gently to combine.
  3. Serve your Champorado hot and enjoy the rich chocolatey goodness!
