Easy Cassava Cake Recipe (Filipino Dessert)

Indulge in this delightful Filipino dessert â€“ a creamy and delicious cassava cake! It's surprisingly easy to make and perfect for any occasion. Serve it chilled for an extra refreshing treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated, peeled yuca
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together yuca, coconut milk, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and eggs until well combined.
  3. Pour the batter into a greased 2-quart baking dish.
  4. Bake for 1 hour, or until set. Broil for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the top is golden brown. Let cool completely in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
